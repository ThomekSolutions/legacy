The world loop

Leave the haven, descend, return changed.

Haven

A monster-free hub where every heir begins before stepping onto the portal tile that opens Depth 1.

Shared Depths

Combat maps scale by depth, cycle through dark biomes, and keep exact portal tiles for forward and return travel.

Monsters

Ruin crawlers, marsh maws, grave wraiths, and cinder brutes grow harsher as the descent deepens.

Permanent death

Your character dies. Your house does not.

Each run builds level, gold, kills, and power until the world takes the heir back. Death creates a grave, adds renown to the house, and may leave a relic behind for the Hall of Legends.
